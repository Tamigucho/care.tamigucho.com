Tamigucho Care app - Soon


In 2016, the original plan was to use pixelated graphics. As of 2023, we'll move to cartoonish graphics before getting ready for launching.

Features

🧬 Fused Breeding 🧬

=

Bring 2 tamiguchos (of the same shape/egg-group), reproduce and get a completely new creature! Unexpected and wonderful (or thrilling) things can happen. Then breed them again to create the new out of the new! 👩‍🔬🧪🔬

Mendel's Laws (Mendelian inheritance) applies.

Other features to be announced. Last update: Thu Aug 24 21:16 2023 -0300.

More info

  • 7 creatures to take care of - and this number will steadily grow to 250, with bi-weekly additions. Reproduce them and trade with friends!
  • Platforms: Phone (Android/iOS/KaiOS), Switch (by porting HTML5 to Native using Ratalaika Games' service)
  • Engine: Construct 2/3

As of Aug 24 2023, Tamigucho Care wasn't launched yet.
